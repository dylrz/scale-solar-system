About This Site

This site is a visual simulation of the solar system, including the planets and other significant objects like Voyager 1. It demonstrates the beauty and scale of our solar system and beyond. More objects are being added, so check back for more!

In this model, a mere 16 seconds represents a year in the endless dance of time. If you watch long enough, you'll see the subtle elliptic nature of the planets' orbits, a result of the gravity the celestial bodies exert on one another. We are all connected in the vast expanse of space.

Enjoy exploring!
